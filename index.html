UWB Technology

Ultra-Wideband Real-Time Location System for Industrial Applications

What is RTLS?

Real-Time Locating System (RTLS) is mainly used to identify and locate the position of people, assets and other objects in real time, usually used within a building, factory or similar contained areas.

Our RTLS solution provides unprecedented visibility into your operations, enabling data-driven decisions and improved safety protocols.

How does UWB RTLS work?

The UWB RTLS is the most precise and reliable indoor location technology available. It uses fixed anchors to measure multiple distances with mobile tags, and a computer calculates the exact position of the tag in real-time.

Distances are evaluated using the propagation speed of short radio signals, which is extremely fast and precise, delivering centimeter-level accuracy.

Industrial RTLS Applications

Since GPS or similar outdoor location systems cannot be used indoors, UWB technology is the best alternative for industrial RTLS applications. UWB tags are highly immune to structural and surrounding materials present in factories or warehouses.

During normal operations, positioning of people, forklifts and important assets provides great insight for understanding the true flow of operations. This observability enables better planning and increased productivity. Tracking production flow and asset lifecycle yields solid KPIs and overall system ratings, helping identify bottlenecks and utilize idle resources just-in-time.

Emergency Response

Last known locations of personnel during search and rescue operations can be crucial. In case of fire and other unexpected situations, people can be trapped or lose consciousness, unable to mobilize themselves or respond to rescue attempts.

RTLS provides the latest acquired location data which greatly reduces search effort and shortens the most critical response time, potentially saving lives in emergency situations.
